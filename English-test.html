Audio Script & Dialogue Reading Comprehension

Title: Meeting a New Neighbor

Emma: Hi, I don't think we've met before. I'm Emma. I just moved into the apartment next door.

David: Nice to meet you, Emma. I'm David. Welcome to the building.

Emma: Thank you. Everyone has been very friendly so far. Could you tell me where the nearest supermarket is?

David: Of course. There's one about five minutes away on King Street. There's also a pharmacy next to it.

Emma: That's helpful. I'm still learning my way around the neighborhood.

David: If you need anything, just let me know. We've all lived here for several years.

Emma: I appreciate that. I'm looking forward to getting to know everyone.

Part A – Multiple Choice

Where is the nearest supermarket?

Part B – True or False
